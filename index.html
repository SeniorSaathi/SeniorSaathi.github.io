About Us

Welcome to Senior Saathi, your personalized career compass! We're dedicated to guiding college students through their career journeys. Our platform offers tailored guidance based on interests and expertise levels. For free, access curated career roadmaps, skill recommendations, and project advice. Upgrade for one-on-one sessions with industry experts, including mock interviews and resume reviews. Empowering you to make informed career choices and excel in your chosen field. Join us at Senior Saathi and navigate your path to success!
